If you do not use your Bluechipcrypto-exchange Card for 90 days, a $5.00 USD dormancy fee is charged each month until you use your Bluechipcrypto-exchange Card is again.

To avoid a dormancy fee, make a purchase using your Bluechipcrypto-exchange Card at least once every 90 days. You can use your Bluechipcrypto-exchange Card almost anywhere VISA is accepted.

If your account becomes dormant and you incur dormancy fees, your account balance will never become negative.

For example, if your balance is $6.00 USD when the account becomes dormant, $5.00 USD will be debited the first month and another $1.00 USD will be debited the next month if no deposit is made. If you have $2.00 USD in your Bluechipcrypto-exchange card account and you incur a $5.00 USD dormancy fee, your account will only be debited $2.00 USD, bringing your balance to $0.00 USD. Your Bluechipcrypto-exchange Card balance will not become negative because of dormancy fees. After you load funds onto your Bluechipcrypto-exchange card again, the system will not collect the balance of the dormancy fee that it was previously unable to collect.

Privacy

Bluechipcrypto-exchange has gone to great lengths to ensure your data and transactions are protected and remain private for your eyes only.
